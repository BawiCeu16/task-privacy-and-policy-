This Privacy Policy explains how Task App (the "App") handles your information and ensures your privacy and security while using our services.

1. Open Source Information

The app is open-source and free to use:

  • Transparency — anyone can inspect the code
  • No hidden costs or fees
  • Community contributions welcome to improve the app

2. No Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your information remains securely on your device. We do not track usage or share any data with third parties.

3. No Ads or Tracking

  • No advertisements
  • No analytics or tracking tools
  • Your usage remains completely private

4. Data Storage & Security

All tasks, notes, and app settings are stored locally on your device. Data is encrypted to ensure safety. If you uninstall the app, all stored data is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

5. App Permissions

The app requires no special permissions such as camera, contacts, or location. It only accesses local storage to save your tasks and preferences.

6. User Responsibility

Since the app stores data locally, users are responsible for backups if needed. Ensure your device is secure to prevent accidental loss of tasks or settings.

7. Security Practices

We follow best practices to ensure that the code is safe and free from vulnerabilities. Community reviews help maintain transparency and security.

8. Updates to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may change in the future. Any updates will be reflected on this page. We encourage users to review it periodically.
